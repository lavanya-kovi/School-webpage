Overview

Experience the wonderful process of enrolling in one of Karnataka's government institutions, as wacky alerts from the State Education Department lead prospective students through qualifying requirements and magical application procedures. The selection process, often a mystical dance choreographed by a lottery system, may favor economically weaker sections, turning admission into a quest for equality.

Some schools may request a modest admission fee, unlocking the door to a world of knowledge. Selected candidates undergo a whimsical verification process, ensuring they possess the essence to thrive in the enchanted realm of education. Admitted students, apprentices in the school of wonders, are urged to acquaint themselves with school rhythms, curriculum melodies, and harmonious rules.

Aspiring wizards and scholars should consult wise elders at the official education department or enchanting schools for the latest information scrolls.

. . . offers diverse Arts, Commerce, and Science courses with 388 students, 11 faculty members, and courses in Science (PCMB/PCMC), Commerce (HEBA, CEBA), and Arts (HEPS).

Rajanakunte's Govt High School

. . . nurtures 197 students in 9th and 10th grades, with a dedicated faculty of 9 in both Kannada and English mediums.

Rajanakunte Govt Primary School

. . . serves students in Std 1-8 with Kannada as the medium for Std 1-5 and both Kannada and English for 6th-8th. 357 students, 5 faculty, and guest faculty contribute to the vibrant learning environment.
