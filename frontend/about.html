About Chairperson

Discover the driving force behind our educational legacy. Our esteemed Chairperson, the visionary architect who initiated our institutions, continues to inspire a culture of excellence and innovation in education.

Shri S R Vishwanath

MLA, Yelahanka | Member, TTD Board
Former Chairman, Bangalore Development Authority (BDA)

Singanayakanahalli Ramaiah Vishwanath, an accomplished Indian politician and current MLA of Yelahanka constituency since 2008, has left an indelible mark on various fronts. Former Chairman of the Bangalore Development Authority and current Member of the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) Board, he demonstrates a diverse commitment to community development.

As chairperson of the Government school and PU college in Rajanukunte, his impact on education is profound. Founder of the Vishwa Vani Foundation, he epitomizes philanthropy and envisions a progressive society. Vishwanath's unwavering dedication to public service and numerous contributions make him a transformative leader inspiring positive change.
